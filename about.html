Forever Learning

I am Abhishek from Mumbai, India. Yes, I shall put down some of the much cliched 'The spirit of Mumbai' here stuff about the city because I believe in it and I am proud of my city. Mumbai and therefore 'Mumbaikars' (a cool demonym for the people of Mumbai) never ever give up!

I moved to Atlanta in 2015 to study Computer Science at GaTech and I have been loving the Grad Student life so far. #wecandothat (That's what we say at Tech at difficult things.)

As you must have guessed from the rest of my website, I am a student who is very passionate about technology and who firmly believes in the transformative power of technology and Computer Science to make this world a better place. What you probably don't know about me is I am obsessed with good music! I listen to a huge variety of music and can have conversations for hours about my favorite bands! I love blues, classic rock and Hindustani Classical music in particular. I pride myself on being able to recognize Indian Ragas and I absolutely adore Anoushka Shankar

I play the guitar pretty badly but that does not stop me jamming alongside better players. If you would like to jam with me, hit me up!

Traveling is another passion of mine, I have traveled extensively in India and I dream of backpacking around many other places of the world. I used to be a voracious reader as a child and continue to read whenever I have time. I am currently reading Elon Musk by Ashlee Vance.

I speak English, Marathi, Hindi and Bengali.
